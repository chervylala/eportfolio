    • Kantin at The Granary

    The creative integration of Sarawak and Italy.

    With catchy names that capture their essence, each dish showcases unique Sarawakian flavors. The chefs source ingredients from bustling markets and hidden rainforests, using unconventional jungle finds and special herbs to create culinary masterpieces.

    Sin Chong Choon

    Kuching local delicacies parked in the ever-filled kopitiam.

    From laksa sarawak to roti kawin, this place is filled with the go-to breakfast options of Kuchingites. Yes, breakfast because they close at 1.30PM. When you are in a rush to try all of our food, their stalls got you covered as they handmade their laksa broth, hand-stretched their Teh C Peng, and welcome you with limitless options.
